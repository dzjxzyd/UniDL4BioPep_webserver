Large-scale output version: 1. Prepare your files (xls, xlsx, fasta, or txt) and click “Choose File” for uploading → 2. Choose a model for classification  → 3. Download the results.


Usage of the webserver:

STEP 1. Select a prediction model based on their index, 1. ACE inhibitory activity; 2. DPP IV inhibitory activity; 3. Bitter; 4. Umami; 5.Antimicrobial activity; 6. Antimalarial activity (main dataset); 7. Antimalarial activity (alternative dataset); 8. Quorum sensing activity; 9. Anticancer activity (main dataset); 10. Anticancer activity (alternative dataset); 11. Anti-MRSA strains activity; 12. Tumor T cell antigens STEP 2. Input your peptide sequence and separate them by comma

Example: you can input 1 in the first box and AGRI,CCL,FIRST in the second box; Than means you want to use "ACE inhibitory activity prediction model" to predict the bioactivity of the four peptide sequences
