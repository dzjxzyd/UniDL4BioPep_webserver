UniDL4BioPep: A universal deep learning architecture for bioactive peptide prediction

The webserver is the implementation of the paper UniDL4BioPep: UniDL4BioPep: A universal deep learning architecture for binary classification in peptide bioactivity

Usage of the webserver:

1. Select a prediction model based on their index, 1. ACE inhibitory activity; 2. DPP IV inhibitory activity; 3. Bitter; 4. Umami; 5.Antimicrobial activity; 6. Antimalarial activity (main dataset); 7. Antimalarial activity (alternative dataset); 8. Quorum sensing activity; 9. Anticancer activity (main dataset); 10. Anticancer activity (alternative dataset); 11. Anti-MRSA strains activity; 12. Tumor T cell antigens
2. Input your peptide sequence and separate them by comma
Example: you can input 1 in the first box and ARG,IPI,CCL,FIRST; Than means you want to use "ACE inhibitory activity prediction model" to predict the bioactivity of the four peptide sequences
